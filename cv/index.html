Publications

  • 2024.11.19
    A comprehensive review of existing corpora and methods for creating annotated corpora for event extraction tasks
    Sciendo
    This study provides a detailed review of annotated corpora for event extraction, essential for improving related algorithms. It aims to guide researchers in developing accurate annotated corpora, offering structured guidelines and tools for corpus preparation. This review highlights the global applicability of English corpora, serving as a valuable framework for improving annotation quality across diverse domains.
  • 2023.01.30
    Systematic Literature Review of Information Extraction From Textual Data: Recent Methods, Applications, Trends, and Challenges
    IEEE
    This study reviews recent advancements in information extraction (IE) techniques, especially for processing textual data. This SLR analyzed 161 recent studies published from 2017 to 2022 provides a focus on core concepts, recent methods, applications, trends, challenges, and future directions in IE from text data. It highlights deep learning models as the state-of-the-art for extracting information from textual sources.
